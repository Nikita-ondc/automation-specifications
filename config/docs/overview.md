# ONDC:FIS13 2.0.1 — Overview

## Summary

ONDC:FIS13 enables consumers to discover, purchase, renew, and manage health and motor insurance policies through ONDC marketplaces and insurance agencies operating as network participants. This domain brings standardized, interoperable insurance distribution to India's open network, allowing consumers to transact with insurance providers and agents on a common infrastructure rather than in isolated systems.

## Sector & Purpose

**Sector:** Insurance & Financial Inclusion

The Indian insurance market has historically operated through fragmented channels—direct company websites, tied agents, or offline brokers. This domain solves the problem of letting users buy health and motor insurance on ONDC, democratizing access by enabling insurance products to be discoverable and purchasable on an open, multi-participant network. It extends ONDC's vision of open commerce to financial services, specifically consumer insurance products.

## Real-World Actors

- **Consumers:** Individuals and families seeking to purchase or manage health and motor insurance policies
- **ONDC BAP (Buyer App Platform):** Digital marketplaces and platforms that aggregate insurance offerings and present them to consumers
- **Insurance Agencies (ONDC BPP):** Licensed insurance agencies, intermediaries, and underwriters who offer policies and process transactions on the ONDC network

## Use Cases

- **Health Insurance Purchase:** Individual or family health insurance application and enrollment
- **Health Insurance Claims:** Filing and processing claims against active health policies
- **Health Insurance Renewal:** Renewing expiring health insurance policies
- **Health Insurance Cancellation:** Terminating active health insurance policies
- **Motor Insurance Purchase:** Individual motor insurance application and enrollment
- **Motor Insurance Claims:** Filing and processing claims against active motor policies
- **Motor Insurance Cancellation:** Terminating active motor insurance policies
- **Pre-Order & Manual Review:** Insurance applications that require underwriting review before confirmation

## Key Concepts

- **Individual and Family Plans:** Insurance products tailored for single individuals or family groups, requiring different underwriting and premium structures
- **Complete Policy Lifecycle:** Support for application, active policy management (claims, renewal), and cancellation—enabling end-to-end transactions
- **Pre-Order & Underwriting Flow:** Policies can be sold conditionally (pre-order) pending manual review by the insurer, allowing underwriting decisions to happen post-purchase confirmation
- **Multi-Product Distribution:** A single network integration supports both health and motor insurance, reducing marketplace fragmentation
- **Standards-Based Agency Model:** Licensed insurance agencies distribute policies through the ONDC network, maintaining regulatory compliance while enabling interoperability

## Example Scenario

A consumer discovers health insurance options on an ONDC marketplace app. They select a family plan from a particular agency partner, provide applicant details, and submit the application. The agency (BPP) receives the application, may perform a manual underwriting review if required by policy rules, and confirms enrollment. Over the policy term, if the consumer needs to file a claim, they initiate the claim flow through the same ONDC app, providing claim details and supporting documentation. The agency processes the claim and communicates the outcome back through the network. When the policy nears expiration, the consumer can renew it through the marketplace, or if no longer needed, cancel it. The entire journey—application, claims, renewal, cancellation—happens on a single ONDC network rather than requiring the consumer to visit multiple company websites or offices.
